A ‘HOTSPOT’ FOR APPAREL SOURCING

Bangladesh is the 3rd largest supplier of Apparels in US according to US official data. Its competitiveness as a supplier is growing due to labor cost and improvements in productivity, infrastructure and Govt incentives for the exporters. Also developments in China’s economy, makes Bangladesh more attractive as a ‘Source’ for Apparel products.

In a recent survey, Mckinsey research found that Bangladesh will be one of the ‘HOTSPOTS’ for Apparel products sourcing. 89% of the 28 major Apparel buyers in US and Europe said that they see Bangladesh among the top 3 sourcing destinations.
